Get Started in Four Easy Steps.

1. Create Your Workspace

Create your own container-based development workspace on our platform. You have complete control over the infrasturcture, tools,frameworks and programming languages that you use. Because our platform is built on Cloud Native tools like Kubernetes your creations are guaranteed to be portable and consistent throughout your development lifecycle.

2. Create Your Application

You can use the command line interface or web console to create and deploy your container-based application in just a few minutes. Jumpstart your application by choosing one of our Intstant Apps, Quickstart Application Templates, AWS Service Components or Builders. Our platform will automatically pull your source code or container image from your repositories, build containers and deploy your application on our internal Kubernetes engine so you can test your application.

3. Manage Your Application in a Cloud Native Development Environment

Monitoring and managing container-based applications require tools purpose built for container development. FaktorZ as developed a full catalog of Cloud Native, open source tools that you can configure, deploy and begin using in your development environment in just a few minutes.

4. Deploy Your Application to Your Favorite Production Environment.

Whether it is to save money or get the best performance out of your production infrastructure, you want to maintain control of where and when your applications get deployed to production. Our partnership with with leading Cloud Native companies and solutions guarantees that your application will never get locked in to expensive, proprietary infrastructure.
